Private IP Address Ranges:

Private IP addes are defined by the
internet standards and fall into specific ranges within the
IPv4 address space. These ranges are reserved for private use,
are not allocated or routable on the Public internet. The reserved
ranges r:

1) Class A Private Range:

Range: 10.0.0.0 to 10.255.255.255
no. of addresses: ≈ 16.7 million

172.16.0.0 to 172.31.255.255
